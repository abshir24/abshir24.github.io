Real Estate Agents

NEXME does not hire agents. Real estate agents must hang their license with your current brokerage firm

Become a NEXME agent partner

NEXME requires real estate agents to have at least 2 years’ experience and 10 or more listing transactions.

By signing up, real estate agents are committing to 1% commission fee, no exceptions.

As a NEXME agent partner, do not ask homebuyers to sign a contract with you until they’re ready to make an offer on one particular home. If that home falls through, the contract ends immediately on that home.
